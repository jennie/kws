## Marketing Coordinator

Part-time: 15 hours/week. $24/hour. Start date: Tuesday, July 28, 2026.

The KWS is hiring a part-time Marketing Coordinator to support audience development and communications during a period of growth. Reporting to the Communications Manager and Executive Director, you will coordinate campaigns, manage our digital communications, and keep our brand consistent across platforms.

### Responsibilities

- Coordinate marketing campaigns across social media, email, ticket sales, fundraising, and subscriber growth
- Track analytics and analyze audience development
- Manage digital communications, including website updates and social media
- Handle media relations and draft press releases
- Maintain brand consistency and coordinate content across platforms
- Assist with print and production for programs and signage
- Support partnership development and community engagement

### Qualifications

- Experience in marketing, communications, arts administration, public relations, or a related field
- Strong written and verbal communication
- Familiarity with social media and email marketing platforms
- Strong organizational and multitasking skills
- Experience with Google Workspace
- Understanding of audience analytics and reporting

Arts sector experience, website management and SEO knowledge, graphic design skills, and media production experience are assets.

### How to apply

Send a single PDF to [info@kwsymphony.com](mailto:info@kwsymphony.com?subject=Marketing%20Coordinator) with the subject line "Marketing Coordinator". Include your cover letter, resume, and two references.

Application deadline: Friday, June 26, 2026 at 12:00 PM (noon).
